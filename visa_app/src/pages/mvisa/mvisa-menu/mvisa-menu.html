MVisa Safety

1. Keep your username and PIN confidential at all times

2. Never store your username and PIN in your phone.

3. Notify DCB Digital in case of :
a. suspicious log in in your app
b. in case of suspicious transaction in your account

4. Only download or upgrade DCB Digital App through Google Play Store and Apple Store.

5. Once you have completed your transaction, log out of DCB Digital app before closing the app.

6. Access DCB Digital App on secure wireless network. Do not use public Wi-Fi hotspots. Unsecure networks can expose sensitive data, making it vulnerable to hackers.
